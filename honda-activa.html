Activa 6G Overview

Honda Activa 6G is a scooter available at a starting price of Rs. 73,181 in India. It is available in 3 variants and 11 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 76,857. The Honda Activa 6G is powered by 109.51cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 7.68 bhp and a torque of 8.79 Nm. With both front and rear drum brakes, Honda Activa 6G comes up with combined braking system of both wheels. This Activa 6G scooter weighs 107 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 5.3 liters.
