Yamaha MT 03 Overview

Yamaha MT 03 is expected to launch in India in October 2022 in the expected price range of ₹ 2,80,000 to ₹ 3,00,000. Currently available bikes which are similar to MT 03 are Honda CB300R, BMW G 310 R & KTM 390 Duke. The Yamaha MT-03 is the streetfighter version of YZF-R3 supersports motorcycle and is the fourth model in the MT range. A compact and easy to use motorcycle, the MT-03 will lock horns with the likes of the Kawasaki Z250, Benelli TNT 300 and the KTM Duke 390.
