Vespa SXL 150 Overview

Vespa SXL 150 is a scooter available at a starting price of Rs. 1,48,261 in India. It is available in 2 variants and 6 colours with top variant price starting from Rs. 1,53,728. The Vespa SXL 150 is powered by 149.5cc BS6 engine which develops a power of 10.32 bhp and a torque of 10.60 Nm. With front disc and rear drum brakes, Vespa SXL 150 comes up with anti-locking braking system. This SXL 150 scooter weighs 114 kg and has a fuel tank capacity of 7.4 liters.

Vespa scooters are sold by the Italian manufacturer, Piaggio, in India. The brand’s portfolio has a range of scooters, all of which feature a nearly identical design and cycle parts. They are available in two engine configurations including 125cc and 150cc models, with the latter being available in two variants – VXL 150 and SXL 150. Here, the SXL is a more premium trim with a rectangular headlamp, mirrors, and fancy matte-finished colour options
